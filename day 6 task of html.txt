1. Purpose of <form> in HTML?

Ans: The <form> element is used to collect user input and send it to a server.

Key attributes:

	action → URL where data is sent

	method → How data is sent (GET or POST)

	<form action="/submit" method="POST">
  	<!-- form inputs go here -->
	</form>

7. HTML5 Validation?

Ams: Built-in validation includes:

	type="email" → checks email format

	required → must be filled

	min / max → number limits

	<label for="email">Email</label>
    	<input type="email" id="email" name="email" placeholder="Enter your email" required maxlength="50" minlength="10"/>
